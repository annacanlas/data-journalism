Data Journalism - Project Pitch


1. Do these journalists spend too much time on Twitter?



Because of its pithiness and immediacy, Twitter has become a favorite medium among journalists. In fact, some post every few minutes that I want to do a word count of all their tweets, compare it to their published articles and see if in the time they spent tweeting, they could have already finished the equivalent of one or several stories.

Form: An infograph that a) compares selected journalists' Twitter word count vs. story word count and b) expresses their Twitter word count in terms of the number of stories they could have written (based on the average word count in their articles).

Notes: B) could be hidden under a mouse hover. Because I am focusing on writers, I shall exclude broadcast journalists from the list.
